# Supply Chain Intelligence Dashboard

## Overview
This project analyzes supply chain performance focusing on inventory management, demand-supply balance, supplier efficiency, and product profitability.

## Objectives
- Identify stock risks and overstock situations
- Analyze demand vs procurement efficiency
- Evaluate supplier performance
- Understand product profitability

## Tools Used
- SQL (Data Analysis)
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
- Excel (Data Preparation)

## Dashboard Features

### Page 1: Inventory & Procurement
- Inventory health (Days Remaining)
- Demand vs Supply mismatch
- Overstock and critical stock insights

### Page 2: Supplier & Profitability
- Supplier performance analysis
- Profit vs Revenue scatter analysis
- Business insights and recommendations

## Key Insights
- 15% products are at critical stock levels
- 70% products are overstocked
- Supplier delays impact inventory availability
- Some products generate high revenue but low profit

## Business Recommendations
- Optimize procurement planning
- Reduce overstock inventory
- Improve supplier selection
- Focus on high-margin products
